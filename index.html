Welcome To Ace Kennels

Ace Kennels is a loving, safe, spacious, full-service dog boarding & daycare facility, that is open to all breeds. Along with facilities rental, training and a large breed rescue, Keshet is a resort for dogs; one that will provide owners with peace of mind.

BOARDING/DAYCARE

Keshet provides an exciting boarding experience in a safe, clean and welcoming environment.

RESCUE & ADOPTION

Keshet Rescue offers the promise of a brighter future for large breed dogs in need of forever homes.

TRAINING/SEMINARS

In conjunction with associates, Keshet provides dogs and their owners with on-going education and tools for success.

FACILITIES RENTAL

Keshet offers a variety of indoor and outdoor spaces to give you a secure environment to interact with your dog(s).
