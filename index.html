Welcome to Keshet Kennels/Rescue

Keshet Kennels is a loving, safe, spacious, full-service dog boarding & daycare facility, that is open to all breeds. Along with facilities rental, training and a large breed rescue, Keshet is a resort for dogs; one that will provide owners with peace of mind.
